# About COVID-19

~ forward from：Wikipedia ~

The COVID-19 pandemic, also known as the coronavirus pandemic, is an ongoing pandemic of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2), first identified in December 2019 in Wuhan, China.
The World Health Organisation declared the outbreak a Public Health Emergency of International Concern in January 2020 and a pandemic in March 2020.
As of 16 November 2020, more than 54.3 million cases have been confirmed, with more than 1.31 million deaths attributed to COVID-19.
